What it includes

A practical stack for scanning, simulation, and operational visibility before any live trading decision.

Realtime scanner

WebSocket orderbook ingestion through ccxt.pro across Binance, KuCoin, OKX, and Bybit.

Paper trading

Fake-money mode uses live market data without sending real orders, making demos and validation safer.

Risk controls

Drawdown limits, loss caps, cooldown, circuit breaker, rate limiting, and slippage tracking.

Safe testing path

New users should validate the bot with fake money first. Live trading should only be enabled after paper results, logs, fees, and exchange permissions are understood.

1. Demo mode

Run the standalone fake-money demo. It reads market data but does not require exchange API keys.

2. Paper trading

Use fake-money mode for normal bot sessions. It records sessions and trades to SQLite for dashboard review.

3. Dry-run guard

Add --dry-run to classic or delta-neutral commands to force the bot onto the FakeMoneyBot path.
